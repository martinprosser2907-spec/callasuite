Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Calla Suite. We are committed to protecting your business and personal data in strict compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver our specialized accounting and single-operator toolkits, we process the following data classes:

  • Account Credentials: Name, identity parameters, phone metrics, and email records.
  • Financial Ledgers: Inbound structural values, expense tracking fields, mileage records, and invoice tracking data.
  • Billing Details: Payment tokens processed exclusively via Stripe's encrypted routing matrix.

2. How We Use Data

We process information to fulfill statutory contracts and maintain your ledger tables, specifically to:

  • Maintain secure profile sessions and compute business structural outgoings.
  • Process OCR parameters for mobile receipt images and invoice components safely.
  • Calculate exact HMRC tax claim allowances down to specified mileage baseline rates.

3. Data Sharing & Processors

We do not lease data arrays to marketing metrics. Information is shared strictly with compliant infrastructure partners needed to run the ecosystem:

  • Stripe: For handling secure, encrypted subscription verification rules.
  • Cloudflare & GitHub: To serve secure, SSL-encrypted content delivery channels.

4. Your Rights

Under UK data laws, you hold clear parameters over your records. You maintain full rights to request immediate access to your data, verify correction structures, or enforce complete deletion rules from our data storage tables at any time.
